MeetU ? But what is it ?

MeetU is a way to quickly and easily retrieve contact information without a business card, phone or other tool in any situation. It's an electronic bracelet allowing you to recover the details of a person met during an event. Simply by "shocking" the bracelets.

The bracelet is worn on various events (festive or professional) as a service. Each participant wears the bracelet until the end the event.
